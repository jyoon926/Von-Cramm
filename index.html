We are Von Cramm

About

With 34 members, Von Cramm Cooperative (the "Cramm") is the largest house owned by Cornell University. We are a diverse group of graduate and undergraduate students with a wide range of backgrounds and interests. Our goal is to be a community that supports and enriches its members through thoughtful interaction, responsibility and generosity. At most times of the day, there are people around to engage in conversation or perhaps a game of pool. Often in winter, people are drawn to the fireplace in the living room, and when warm weather arrives they hang out in the back yard.Learn More
