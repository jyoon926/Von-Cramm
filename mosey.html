Mosey

Membership to reside in Von Cramm is open to all full-time Cornell students, including undergraduate, graduate, international and transfer students. Von Cramm welcomes and strives to provide safe spaces for people of color, queer individuals, students with physical or mental disabilities, students with financial burdens, and students with other marginalized identities.

Semesterly Housing Rates

Our house consists of 19 singles, 6 doubles and 1 triple.

  • Single: $2400 (~$600/month)
  • Double: $2100 (~$525/month)
  • Triple: $1800 (~$450/month)

Selection

Admission to reside in Von Cramm for the following academic year is done via random selection. In order to enter the selection system for a room in VonCramm, all prospective Crammies must submit a questionnaire or have an oral interview with the current president and go through Mosey. Mosey consists of at least:

  • Attending Open House
  • Attending two of the scheduled Mosey events (or possibly arranging their own house tour)
  • Helping prepare one house dinner at Von Cramm

Meal Plan

Our meal plan is run by the members of the meal plan. All house members are required to be a part of the meal plan, but the meal plan is open to members of the Cornell community. Crammies are required to cook on the meal plan unless otherwise stated in the constitution. External meal planners have the option of cooking or paying an additional fee to not cook on the meal plan.

Semesterly Meal Plan Rate



  • Cooking: $510
  • Not Cooking: $510 + Additional fee*

Additional fee ≈ minimum wage × 3.5 hours/week × the number of weeks of the semester’s meal plan. Learn more about the Meal Plan from our constitution here.
